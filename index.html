About the Project

Neon Knight Lab is a futuristic music project focused on electronic sound, cinematic atmosphere, and visual storytelling.

The project combines music, artwork, and automated publishing workflows to build a consistent and scalable creative universe across digital platforms.

Each release is designed not only as a song, but as part of a broader audiovisual identity shaped by technology and artistic direction.
